Intermediate Guide

Mid-Game Guide for CraftersMC Players

As a mid-game player, your primary focus should be unlocking minion slots, not just earning coins. The goal is to unlock 15 minion slots, which requires obtaining 200 unique minions.

1. Craft Minions and Use a Jungle Axe

  • Grind for Oak, Dark Oak, Spruce, Birch, Acacia, and Jungle Minions and upgrade each to Tier 9.
  • Use a Jungle Axe (available for around 5k coins) to speed up wood collection.
  • This will contribute 45 unique minion unlocks.

2. Maximize Your Pumpkin Farm

  • If you already have a Pumpkin Farm, create one maxed-out Pumpkin Minion and sell it.
  • Use the proceeds to fund upgrades for 11 additional minion slots.

3. Build Beetroot and Potato Farms

  • On your island, create Beetroot and Potato Farms.
  • Grind for Enchanted Beetroot and Enchanted Potatoes to craft and upgrade their respective minions to Tier 9.
  • This will unlock 18 unique slots.

4. Visit the Fungi Cave

  • Head to the Fungi Cave to unlock the recipes for Redstone Minions and Lapis Minions.
  • Place these minions on your island and upgrade them to contribute an additional 10 slots.

5. Grind in the Craven Lobby

  • Visit the Craven Lobby to mine Iron, Gold, and Coal.
  • Unlock the recipes for their respective minions, craft them, and place them on your island.
  • Upgrade Redstone, Lapis, Coal, Iron, and Gold Minions to Tier 9.

6. Maximize Cobblestone Minion

  • Grind cobblestone on your island to max out a Cobblestone Minion.

7. Unlock the Duck Minion

  • Grind ducks in Harvest Heaven to unlock the Duck Minion recipe.

8. Ideal Minion Setup

Once you have unlocked 15 minion slots, place the following on your island:

  • 2 Redstone Minions
  • 1 Lapis Minion
  • 2 Coal Minions
  • 2 Iron Minions
  • 2 Gold Minions
  • 2 Duck Minions
  • 2 Dark Oak Minions
  • 2 Oak Minions

With this setup, you will have fully unlocked 15 minion slots, setting you up for further progression in CraftersMC.

    The Bazaar guide

    The Bazaar is an in-game trading system where players can buy and sell materialsdirectly with others. You no longer need to spam chat or wait for buyers/sellers—just list your items or place an order!

    How to Access the Bazaar

    To unlock the Bazaar, you need to have an average skill level of 7 (this includes Farming, Mining, Combat, Foraging, and other skills). Once you meet this requirement, you can access the Bazaar in the following ways:

    • Option 1: Talk to Flick in the Village Center.
    • Option 2: Use the Bazaar-book in the Village Center.
    • Option 3: (For Diamond/Emerald ranks): Use '/bazaar' or '/bz'.

    What Can You Trade?

    • Farming Items: Crops like wheat, potatoes, etc.
    • Mining Items: Resources like emerald, diamonds.
    • Combat Items: Mob drops.
    • Foraging Items: Wood.
    • Oddities: Rare or unique materials.

    Main Features

    1. Sell Offers

    List your items for a price you choose.
    Steps:
    1. Select an item and amount.
    2. Choose your price (lower prices fill faster).
    3. Wait for a buyer or use the "instant sell" option if you're in a hurry.

    2. Buy Orders

    Place an order for items at a price you specify.
    Steps:
    1. Select an item and amount.
    2. Set your price (higher prices fill faster).
    3. Wait for a seller or use the "instant buy" option for quick transactions.

    3. Instant Sell

    Quickly sell items to existing buy orders.
    Note: You’ll get 4% fewer coins upfront to account for price changes, but the full amount will be refunded after the transaction.

    4. Instant Buy

    Quickly buy items from existing sell offers.
    Note: You’ll pay 4% extra upfront to account for price changes, but the extra will be refunded after the transaction.

    Extra Features

    • Average Price: View the 7-day average price of any item to avoid overpaying or underselling.
    • Rank Perks:
      • Diamond/Emerald ranks: Use Bazaar remotely with '/bazaar' or '/bz'.
      • Emerald rank: Mark your favorite items for quick access and use shortcut commands (e.g '/buy wheat 64' will instantly buy 64 wheat).
    Feature Speed Profit/Cost Flexibilty
    Sell offer: Slower (dependent on demand) Higher profit (usually) More flexible, set price and wait
    Buy offer: Slower (dependent on demand) Higher profit (usually) More flexible, set price and wait
    Instant Sell: Fast (slightly bugged at the moment) Lower Not as flexible, bazaar chooses the price
    Instant buy Fast (slightly bugged at the moment) Lower Not as flexible, bazaar chooses the price

    Bazaar Trading Strategies

    1. Profit from Price Fluctuations

    Bazaar Flipping: Buy low and sell high. Use graphs to identify price dips or spikes. Example: Buy Enchanted Rotten Flesh at a low price and create a sell offer at a higher rate.

    2. Bulk Orders for Discounts

    Create buy orders for large quantities instead of buying instantly. This allows you to get items at a lower cost.

    3. Focus on High-Demand Items

    Items like the Super Compactor 3000 often have steady demand and good margins.

    4. Use Presets for Efficiency

    Utilize preset quantities and pricing to save time while creating orders or offers.

    5. Avoid Overpricing

    Keep your offers competitive by matching or slightly undercutting existing prices.

    Best Reforges for Accessories

    Epic Rarity and Above:

    • Strong. Not even a contest. At Epic Rarity, it matches the total STR + CD number of a Forceful or Hurtful, and at Legendary and Special, the STR + CD number is actually higher. Plus, you get extra defense, so why the heck not?
    • If you’re desperate for AS, Strange on Epics.

    Rare Rarity:

    If Strength is Too High:

    • Hurtful or Itchy. Itchy gives a slightly lower total buff, but the increase in AS might be appealing.

    If Crit Damage is Too High:

    • Forceful.

    If Strength and Crit Damage are close to Equal:

    • Alternate between Forceful and Hurtful. It doesn’t matter which one is prioritized.

    Common or Uncommon Rarity:

    If Strength is Too High:

    • Hurtful or Itchy.

    If Crit Damage is Too High:

    • Forceful.

    If Strength and Crit Damage are close to Equal:

    • Superior.

    Uncommons Only:

    If you want to focus on Attack Speed, Strange isn’t a bad pick.

    Commons Only:

    If you want to focus on Crit Chance, go for Unpleasant.

    Queries

    1. At what collection will I get Legendary Rock Pet?

    You need to grind 450k+ blocks to obtain the Legendary Rock Pet. While grinding, you'll also acquire Common, Uncommon, Rare, and Epic Rock Pets. Sell these additional pets for extra coins.

    2. What to do with Enchanted Cobblestone?

    Avoid selling the Enchanted Cobblestone you collect during the grind. Instead, use it to craft Super Compactors and place them in your minions to boost their productivity.

    3. What to do with coins?

    Be cautious with your spending and avoid wasting coins. Save the coins earned from selling Rock Pets to support your future upgrades and progression.
